Digital Designer



Hello there. My name is Juraj Molnár and I am digital designer based in Nitra Slovakia. Besides being a loving husband and full-time father, I love to design things. My biggest design passion belongs to websites and applications, which I've been crafting for over 3 years. Creation of unique and exceptional products for my clients is on my daily basis. I love to cooperate, communicate and deliver the best web and mobile solutions.

I started my career as junior designer and front-end-developer 4 years ago in a small web design agency in Bratislava. After 2 years I moved to eFabrica and take a role of creative Web & UX designer where I work till today. I was honored to work on the biggest media and social projects in the Slovakia. Now I'm also freelancing and cooperating with other creative individuals around the world.
