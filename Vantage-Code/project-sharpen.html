Vibrant Admin bootstrap theme following the latest google material design principles equipped with Material Design Iconic Font. Theme consists from 11 different pages suitable for building mid range content management system.

Design is spacious and elements could be combined in many different ways. You can check and download free title page PSD GUI right here. Feel free to come up with new ideas, subpages and improvements.



Material design


Theme is insipred by a google material design guidelines a big phenomenon these days. Whole design uses consistent typography, colors,paddings and animations for achieving professional and smooth look and preserve the best usability for admin users.


Vibrant Color Scheme


Design is revived with fresh colors from Google Material Palette. Theme comes with more than 18 different fresh colors which could be combined in many different ways. There is also possibility to use own color schemes by modifying a css document.
