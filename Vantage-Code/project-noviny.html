Modern & clean design delivered for one of the biggest news portals in Slovakia. We designed various types of layouts, test it and finally approved the most usable one. Whole site is inlayed into 12 column 990 px grid system.

The website has 5 main branches - noviny, topstar, jojsport, premuza, prezenu. Layout is spacious, clean and easy to scan. Content is built from the latest news aggregated from the CMS. Interconnection of articles is achieved thanks to social media and discussions.



New Flat Design Approach


We took, deconstructed and rebuilt the whole concept and modernize the overall look, usability and function. Website is displayed in easy to read grid and allow you to share, enjoy, comment articles and browse related or top rated stories which helps to increase overall page use. Strong social interconnection was our key success.


Re-designed Weather Interface


Galleries are designed in common way/pattern that people follow on other websites. Each gallery has its own url address so we can easily measure collected data.
